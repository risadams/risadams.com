Need to clear your tempfiles? check here:

C:\Windows\Microsoft.NET\Framework64\v4.0.30319\Temporary ASP.NET Files

Make sure you run IISReset before to clear any locks. and after too, just to rub it in IIS's smarmy little bastard face.
