Don't stop believing

What is a unicorn really?

Clearly a mythical horned creature doesn't really exist: but as a symbol of hope, a unicorn can be anything that you want.

  • A physical object
  • A goal for life, health, career.
  • a romantic partnership

Believing that what you want is an obtainable goal, and having a plan to achieve it is empowering

Being an optimist doesn't always mean that you need to be happy--but that you have hope that the future is bringing something better.

Have hope.

Believe in unicorns.
