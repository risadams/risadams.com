Noel

200? - 3/12/2019

Goodbyes are never easy

Noel. She was the greatest consequence of a bad decision. My Wife and I were buying cat food. We stopped to look at the cats available for rescue and met Noel. She was a scrawny, scared little lady who was found alone on a stretch of highway. The agency told us that they did not expect her to live beyond the week. Today, a little over 3 years later, we say goodbye and good memories to the one of the sweetest cats that I have ever known.

Noel passed away loved, and peacefully while resting in my wife's arms early this morning. We held a private ceremony at home. Our other cats, and our dog are doing well.

She came into our life. And she made it better

We miss you. We love you.
